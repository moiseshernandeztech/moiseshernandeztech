# 👾 About Me

Hi! I'm **Moisés Hernández**, an **Application Support Engineer and SRE** based in Buenos Aires, Argentina 🇦🇷.

I currently work at **JP Morgan**, supporting trading-related applications and focusing on system reliability, observability, monitoring, incident management, and operational excellence.

My focus is on **Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)**, where I apply reliability, observability, automation, and operational excellence principles to support critical systems. I continuously seek opportunities to deepen my expertise, gain hands-on experience with modern cloud and platform technologies, and promote industry best practices across infrastructure and application environments.

I enjoy building tools, automating repetitive tasks, creating dashboards, and learning modern infrastructure technologies.
